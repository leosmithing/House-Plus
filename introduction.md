# HousePlusGroup Factory

HousePlusGroup Factory (also known as HousePlus Ltd) is a China-based manufacturer and supplier specializing in a broad range of consumer electronics (3C goods), solar energy solutions, and household appliances.

## Product Catalog
HousePlus operates as a one-stop solution provider for the following categories:

- **Solar Energy Products:** Inverters (MPPT/PWM controllers), portable solar panels, foldable panels, and solar lithium batteries.

- **3C Goods:** Memory cards (SD/SSD), Bluetooth headphones/earphones, power banks (including solar variants), and smartwatches.

- **Kitchen Appliances/Household Appliances:** Induction/infrared cookers, breakfast makers, solar fans, and outdoor power supplies.
