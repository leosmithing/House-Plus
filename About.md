# About HousePlusGroup

HousePlusGroup Factory (also known as HousePlus Ltd) is a China-based manufacturer and supplier specializing in a broad range of consumer electronics (3C goods), solar energy solutions, and household appliances.

## House Plus Product Catalog:
House Plus operates as a one-stop solution provider for the following categories:

### House Plus Solar Energy Products:
- Inverters (MPPT/PWM controllers)
- Portable solar panels
- Foldable panels
- Solar lithium batteries.

### House Plus 3C Goods:
- Memory cards (SD/SSD)
- Bluetooth headphones/earphones
- Power banks (including solar variants)
- Smartwatches.

### House Plus Kitchen Appliances/Household Appliances:
- Induction/infrared cookers
- Breakfast makers
- Solar fans
- Outdoor power supplies.
