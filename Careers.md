# Careers

Welcome to the Careers page of House-Plus! We are excited to offer a range of job opportunities across various fields. If you are passionate and driven, we want to hear from you!

## Job Opportunities

### Sales & Marketing Positions
- **Sales Executive**: Responsible for driving sales and managing customer relationships.
- **Marketing Manager**: Overseeing marketing strategies and execution to drive brand visibility and market reach.

### Operations & Logistics
- **Supply Chain Coordinator**: Managing supply chain operations and ensuring efficient delivery of products.
- **Quality Assurance Inspector**: Ensuring products meet quality standards and specifications.

### Engineering & Product Development
- **Product Engineer**: Developing and improving products while collaborating with cross-functional teams.
- **Solar Systems Technician**: Installing and maintaining solar energy systems for various applications.

### Customer Service Positions
- Provide support and assistance to customers, ensuring a positive experience with House-Plus.

### Internship Programs
- We offer a variety of internships across departments to help students and recent graduates gain valuable experience.

If you are interested in applying for any of these positions or internships, please send your resume to careers@house-plus.com!
